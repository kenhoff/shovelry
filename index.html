For Shovelry!

Shovelry.js is a tiny library to add Shovel Knight-themed strong and emphasis text to any page.

Usage


<script src="shovelry.js" charset="utf-8"></script>
<link rel="stylesheet" href="shovelry.css">

<p>
    Why is it called the <em>Dark Ages</em>?
    Because there's no shortage of <strong>knights!</strong>"
</p>

<script type="text/javascript">
    shovelry();
</script>

FAQ

Does it work with non-monospaced text?

Sure does! Serifed, sans-serifed, fantasy, cursive, even Comic Sans!

Why does mine still have the bold/italics on the text?

I've pulled off the `font-style` and `font-weight` styles on this page to make it a little more Shovel Knight-ly. You can do it yourself by just setting some `em { font-style: normal; }` and `strong { font-weight: normal; }` styles on your page.
